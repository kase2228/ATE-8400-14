Our Hospital Rooms

Our 80 hospital rooms are designed to provide a comfortable and welcoming environment for our patients. We understand that being in the hospital can be a stressful and sometimes scary experience, which is why we have created rooms that promote healing and relaxation.

Our rooms are designed to accommodate different patient needs. All of our rooms are cleaned and sanitized regularly to ensure that they remain sterile and free of germs.

We also offer a range of amenities to make your stay in the hospital as comfortable as possible. Our rooms are equipped with adjustable beds, comfortable seating, and ample space to move around.
